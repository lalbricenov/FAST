Elevation data FAST

From the dataset wsc_elevation.csv, several visualizations were made. This is a work in progress.

  • Elevation data. You can click on the legends to filter by day, and use the mouse to zoom in and pan.
  • Gradient data. You can click on the legends to filter by day, and use the mouse to zoom in and pan.
  • Route with gradient colors and daily stops. Zoom in to view properly.
